Redesign for the non profit organization My Friends House. Our objective was to help create a calm, seamless experience for users to find the help they need.

Project Overview

The Proposal

To redesign the My Friend's House website to helpcreate a calm, seamless experience for victims to find the help they need. We understand that victims may be under a lot of stress so want to make the process as easy as possible for them. We want to ensure that the website doesn't overemphasize that the user is a victim.

Problem Statement

How might we redesign Myfriendshouse.ca so that abused women and their children can easily and safely gain access to information and resources they need to make about their lives and that of their children.Myfriendshouse was redesigned to streamline providing vital information and serving as a safe space for women and their children. We have observed that there are many situations where women are not in a safe space to look for information or resources they need to better their life. This results in a continuous cycle of living in abusive situations that don't allow them to make informed decisions to better their lives.
